Disaster Response Partners

Federal agencies and nonprofits that provide geospatial data, imagery, and coordination during disasters.

Federal / Coordination

FEMA Geospatial Resource Center

Shared hub for disaster response data, maps, and apps. Aggregates content from FEMA, HHS, Census, NASA, and other partners. Decision-makers at every level access this before, during, and after disasters. Hosts Geospatial Coordination Calls during large-scale events.

Resource Center Coordination Calls
Nonprofit / GIS Adoption

NAPSG Foundation

National Alliance for Public Safety GIS. A 501(c)(3) nonprofit since 2005 helping federal, tribal, state, and local public safety agencies adopt GIS as a tool to protect citizens. Bridges the gap between technology and agency readiness.

Website
Esri / Free Support

Esri Disaster Response Program

Esri provides free GIS technology and disaster response expertise when an organization's capacity is exceeded. Your escalation path when a disaster is bigger than your team can map alone. Part of Esri's corporate citizenship initiative.

DRP Portal Request Help
NASA / Satellite Data

NASA Disasters Mapping Portal

Near real-time and disaster-specific data products in GIS format. Satellite imagery, flood extents, fire perimeters, and more. Downloads directly in GIS-ready formats for ArcGIS workflows. Supports prediction, preparation, response, and recovery.

Mapping Portal Program Info
Volunteer / GIS Missions

URISA GISCorps

Short-term volunteer GIS services for organizations and communities in need worldwide. Hundreds of missions since 2003 across 80+ countries. Humanitarian relief, public health, conservation. Potential surge capacity for Red Cross disaster events.

GISCorps Active Missions
Federal / Emergency Imagery

USGS Emergency Response

Rapid access to geospatial imagery and products before, during, and after disasters. Aerial and satellite imagery for pre/post disaster comparisons. Maintains tools and dashboards specifically for the response community.

USGS Emergency Tools & Dashboards
Federal / Intelligence

National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency

World-class geospatial intelligence for military, intelligence, and first responders. Also supports humanitarian assistance and disaster relief. Resources include aeronautical charts, declassified satellite imagery, historical imagery, and open-source GitHub tools.

NGA GitHub HADR

Data Sources

Earth science, hazard, and geospatial datasets for analysis and planning.

Earth, water, biological, and mapping data from the United States Geological Survey. Geography and mapping resources, natural hazards data, water and flood information, plus educational programs like Data in Schools and Adventures in STEM.

Publicly available maps and geographic publications from the Central Intelligence Agency. Useful reference materials for international disaster response and geographic context.

CIA Maps

Historical satellite imagery that has been declassified and made available to the public. Valuable for historical land use analysis, baseline comparisons, and understanding long-term environmental change.

Declassified Archive
